The Hard Problems

1. Real-Time Portfolio Risk

Calculate margin across thousands of correlated derivatives positions in sub-second latency. Margin must reflect portfolio-level offsets, not just isolated instruments. Current CCPs: 45-90 minutes. We need: <800ms.

2. Dual-Mode Settlement

Build settlement infrastructure that works identically across blockchain (Canton/Daml) and traditional rails (FIX), with atomicity guarantees on both paths. Same risk engine, same ledger, different execution layers.

3. Default Isolation

One counterparty failure must have zero impact on any other counterparty's positions or collateral. This requires bilateral netting, real-time risk monitoring, and instant tail risk externalization to capital markets.
