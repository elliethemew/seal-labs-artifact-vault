Vietnam Specific Insight

The "Anxious Builder" & The Leapfrog Effect

1. The "Faceless" Creator Economy

Vietnamese youth are leveraging AI not just for homework, but for wealth generation. A massive trend of "Faceless" YouTube/TikTok channels uses ChatGPT for scripts, Midjourney for visuals, and CapCut AI for editing. This allows introverted youth to enter the global creator economy, earning USD while staying anonymous.
